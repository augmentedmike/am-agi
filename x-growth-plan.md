## Reply-Guy Daily Routine

**The reply-guy strategy is the #1 lever for zero-follower accounts.** At 0 followers, your original content has no distribution. Replies piggyback on existing audiences.

### Target Tier Breakdown (30–50 replies/day)

| Tier | Account Size | Replies/Day | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Big Fish | 50K+ followers | 10–15 | Maximum visibility; even 1–2% attention = hundreds of profile views |
| Mid-tier | 5K–50K | 10–15 | Relationship building; these accounts often reply back |
| Peer | 500–5K | 5–10 | Mutual growth; they become advocates |
| Rising | <500 | 3–5 | Pay it forward; they remember who was early |

### Reply Formula

> **[Personal connection or relevant experience] + [Unique insight or angle] + [One question or implicit invitation to respond]**

**Examples:**
- ❌ Bad: "Great point! Totally agree."
- ❌ Bad: "This. 🙏"
- ✅ Good: "Ran into this exact problem deploying GPT-4o last month — the latency spike was worse than the accuracy drop. Did you find the temperature threshold mattered, or was it token count?"
- ✅ Good: "The framing here is underrated. Most people optimize for benchmark score, not production reliability. That gap is what's going to bite teams in 2027."

### Rate Limits (Hard Stops)
- Max 30 replies/hour
- Max ~150 replies/day before platform flags
- Space replies — don't fire 10 in 2 minutes
- No copy-paste; every reply must be unique

### Reply Session Setup
- Use a desktop browser (faster, better ergonomics)
- Open 15–20 tabs before starting (accounts to reply to)
- Sort by "Latest" on big accounts to catch posts before they get buried
- Aim for posts <30 minutes old — early replies surface highest

## Thread Hook Formulas (4+ Examples)

Threads are 3–5× more engaging than single posts. The hook (first post) determines whether anyone reads past line 1.

### Formula 1: Counterintuitive Statement
> **"[Common belief everyone holds] is wrong. Here's what actually works:"**

Example:
> "You don't need to go viral to hit 10K followers. Going viral on the wrong topic can actually hurt you. Thread on why niche coherence beats viral reach every time:"

### Formula 2: Specific Number + Credibility Signal
> **"[Specific action] gave me [specific result] in [specific timeframe]. Here's exactly how:"**

Example:
> "3 things I changed on X that grew my AI account from 0 to 847 followers in 14 days (no paid promotion, no bots):"

### Formula 3: Curiosity Gap
> **"The one [thing] nobody talks about that [dramatic result]:"**

Example:
> "The one X feature 99% of creators ignore — that accounts for 30% of my follower growth. It's not threads, spaces, or communities:"

### Formula 4: Personal Story Arc
> **"I was [struggling state] for [time]. Then I tried [thing]. Here's what happened:"**

Example:
> "I was stuck at 312 followers for 3 months. Same content, same effort, no growth. Then I changed one habit and gained 600 followers in 4 weeks. What changed:"

### Formula 5: The List Promise
> **"[N] [things] about [topic] that most people get wrong:"**

Example:
> "7 things the X algorithm rewards in 2026 that most creators don't know (with signal weights from the source code):"

### Thread Structure Rules
- Post 1: Hook (see above)
- Posts 2–6: One insight per post, each ending with implicit reason to keep reading
- Post 7: Summary + direct call-to-follow ("If this was useful, follow me — I post threads like this weekly")
- Post 8 (optional): Ask a question to drive replies

## Engagement Velocity Checklist (Post-Publish Priming)

The first 15–30 minutes after posting determine whether the algorithm distributes your post to out-of-network users. Prime it immediately.

**Within 2 minutes of posting:**
- [ ] Reply to your own post with a follow-up thought, question, or additional context (creates immediate conversation signal)
- [ ] Bookmark your own post (adds bookmark signal)
- [ ] If it's a thread: engage with the first reply to keep the chain alive

**Within 15 minutes:**
- [ ] DM 2–3 close contacts who are genuinely interested in the topic ("I just posted something you'll probably have thoughts on") — never mass-DM
- [ ] Check if anyone has already replied — respond immediately; two-way conversations are worth 150× algorithmically

**Within 60 minutes:**
- [ ] Reply to every comment on the post, no matter how short
- [ ] If a reply is substantial, quote-tweet it with your response instead of just replying (generates a new distribution event)
- [ ] Pin the thread to your profile if it's performing

**If the post isn't getting traction after 2 hours:**
- [ ] Do NOT delete and repost — shadow penalty applies
- [ ] Move on; redirect engagement energy to replies elsewhere
- [ ] Repurpose the idea into a different format tomorrow

## Do Not Do List

### Follow-Unfollow Tactic
**Never.** This is the #1 shadowban trigger in 2026. X detects the follow→unfollow pattern algorithmically within hours.

### Automation
- No auto-liking, auto-following, auto-retweeting, auto-replying
- No engagement pods (coordinated manual or automated engagement groups)
- No scheduling tools that post on your behalf via API without review — use queue-based tools only (Buffer, Hypefury), not bots
- Scheduling tools for timing posts = fine. Automated engagement = banned.

### Follow Volume Limits
- Week 1: Max 50 follows/day
- Week 2–4: Max 100–150 follows/day
- Never follow >400 accounts in a day under any circumstances

### Hashtag Rules
- Use 0–2 hashtags per post maximum
- Never use trending hashtags on unrelated content (explicit shadowban trigger)
- Never hashtag-stuff: "#AI #tech #crypto #productivity #startup #growth #founder"
- Better: no hashtags at all than wrong hashtags

### External Links (Critical Rule for New Accounts)
- Zero external links in posts for the first 7–14 days
- After that: max 1 external link per day
- Links are penalized 40–70% in distribution for new accounts
- Workaround: post the link in a reply to your own post (reduces penalty)

### Generic Replies
- "Great point!" → banned from your vocabulary
- "This." → same
- "Love this!" → same
- Every reply must add a specific thought, data point, or question

### Mass Reporting Bait
- Don't post anything inflammatory just to get engagement — report spam flags can trigger review
- Don't argue in bad faith or chase outrage for impressions in early growth phase

## Shadowban Detection & Recovery

### What Is a Shadowban?
X reduces your distribution (sometimes to near-zero) without notifying you. You can still post, but almost nobody sees it. Common types:
- **Search ban:** Your posts don't show up in search results
- **Ghost ban:** Your replies are invisible to others in conversations
- **Trend ban:** Content excluded from trending topics

### How to Detect
1. Open a private/incognito browser
2. Search your username → if your recent posts don't appear, you have a search ban
3. Ask a non-follower to look at a conversation you replied in → if your reply is invisible to them, you have a ghost ban
4. Tool: https://shadowban.eu (free checker)

### Common Triggers (2026)
- Posting external links excessively on a new account
- Following too aggressively (>200/day)
- Using automation
- Mass-replying with similar text to many accounts
- Getting mass-reported by a community
- Follow-unfollow behavior

### Recovery Protocol
1. **Stop the triggering behavior immediately** — this is the only cure
2. **Do not post** during the suspected shadowban period (posting extends it)
3. Wait 48–72 hours after stopping the trigger
4. **Do not delete posts** — deletion spikes can look suspicious
5. Resume posting conservatively after 72 hours
6. Most bans lift within: 24–48h (search ban), 48–72h (ghost ban)
7. If ban persists after 7 days: appeal via X support → "content distribution issue"
