## Step 0: Pre-Launch Setup (Before Day 1)

### Pick Your Niche
**Recommended: AI/Tech or Crypto/Finance**

Both are over-indexed by Grok's SimClusters routing in 2026:
- **AI/Tech:** X is the primary real-time venue for AI discussion. Grok's semantic routing clusters AI content tightly, meaning one post can reach the entire AI community. Sub-niches: AI tools roundups, AI productivity, building with AI, AI ethics, prompt engineering.

**Why NOT generalist content:** Grok rewards niche coherence. Generalist/lifestyle accounts get the least algorithmic amplification in 2026.

**Pick one niche. Stay in it for all 30 days.**

Find the top 3–5 active Communities in your niche. Post **every piece of content** to at least one Community until you hit 3,000 followers. Communities deliver ~2× higher follow conversion per impression vs standalone posts.

## Week 1 (Days 1–7): Warm-Up

**Goal:** Establish account legitimacy. Avoid bot-detection triggers. Build initial engagement history.

The algorithm treats new high-volume accounts as likely bots. Week 1 is about signaling that you're a real person with genuine interest in a niche.

### Posting Rules — Week 1
- **Volume:** 2–3 posts per day (not 5–10 yet)
- **No external links** — zero. Not in posts, not in replies. New accounts posting links frequently get flagged as spam.
- **No hashtags in Week 1** — they trigger spam detection on new accounts
- **Post times:** 9–11 AM audience local time (Tue–Thu priority)

### Content Mix — Week 1
| Day | Content Type |
|---|---|
| Day 1 | Introduction post (who you are, what you're building, why this niche) |
| Day 2 | Short take / hot opinion in your niche (100–200 chars, no link) |
| Day 3 | First thread (5–7 posts) on a core topic in your niche |
| Day 4 | Observation or question — ask your audience something genuine |
| Day 5 | Reaction/commentary on something trending in your niche |
| Day 6 | Thread or long-form take |
| Day 7 | Recap / reflection post |

### Reply Routine — Week 1
**Target: 20–30 replies per day** (build up; don't hit 50/day immediately)

**Reply tiers:**
- 10 replies on large accounts (50K+ followers) in your niche — for visibility
- 10 replies on medium accounts (5K–50K) — for relationship building
- 5–10 replies on smaller accounts (<5K) — for loyal early followers

**Reply formula:**
> [One sentence connecting to your personal experience/expertise] + [One genuinely valuable insight or addition to what they said] + optionally end with a relevant follow-up question

❌ "Great post! So true!"  
❌ "This is exactly what I needed to hear."  
✅ "I ran into exactly this with [specific context] — what worked for me was [specific tactic]. The part about [X] is underrated."  
✅ "Counterpoint: [specific thing they missed]. Curious if you've tried [specific approach]?"

**Rate limit:** Max 30 replies/hour. Never burst 5+ in quick succession.

### What NOT to Do — Week 1
- Do not follow more than 50–100 accounts total in Week 1
- Do not use follow-unfollow tactics ever
- Do not schedule or automate anything (X detects behavioral patterns)
- Do not copy-paste replies
- Do not repost more than 5–10 times/day

## Weeks 2–4 (Days 8–30): Full Throttle

**Goal:** Maximize follower acquisition through high-frequency content + aggressive reply strategy.

### Posting Schedule — Weeks 2–4

**Daily volume: 3–5 posts per day** (up to 10 during a spike week if you have content)

| Slot | Time | Content Type |
|---|---|---|
| Morning | 9–10 AM | Primary post (thread OR strong standalone take) |
| Midday | 12–1 PM | Short take, question, or reaction |
| Afternoon | 3–5 PM | Optional: secondary thread or engagement bait post |

**Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday  
**Acceptable:** Monday, Friday  
**Avoid:** Saturday, Sunday (lowest engagement across all niches)

### Weekly Content Rhythm
| Week | Focus |
|---|---|
| Week 2 | Establish voice — 2–3 strong threads, daily short takes, first pinned post |
| Week 3 | Double down on what worked — recreate top-performing formats |
| Week 4 | Experiment — try native video (60–90s), carousel images (2–4), giveaway |

### Content Formats by Priority

1. **Threads (5–8 posts):** Your primary follower-acquisition format. Write 2–3 per week.
2. **Short takes (100–280 chars):** Daily. Punchy opinions, counterintuitive claims, specific observations.
3. **Native video (60–90 sec):** Highest algorithmic distribution weight. At least 2 per 30 days.
4. **Carousel images (2–4 images):** Second highest distribution. Use for step-by-step frameworks.
5. **External links:** Max 1 per day after Week 1; share as a reply to your own post instead of in the original tweet when possible (reduces link penalty).

### Thread Hook Formulas

Use these exact structures:

**1. Bold Counterintuitive Claim**
> "You don't need to go viral to hit 10K followers. Here's what actually matters:"

**2. Specific Numbers**
> "3 things I did to gain 847 followers in 14 days (without going viral):"

**3. Curiosity Gap**
> "The one X feature nobody talks about that 10×'d my reach:"

**4. Personal Story**
> "I was stuck at 300 followers for 6 months. One change got me to 1,200 in 30 days. Here's exactly what I did:"

**5. Contrarian Take**
> "Hot take: most X growth advice is wrong. The algorithm rewards the opposite of what everyone tells you:"

**6. Listicle With Stakes**
> "9 things I wish I knew before starting on X. #7 cost me 3 months of growth:"

### Reply Routine — Weeks 2–4

**Target: 40–50 replies per day**

Dedicate 45–60 minutes daily to replies. Split into two sessions:
- Morning (30 min) — right after posting your morning content
- Evening (15–30 min) — catch threads that spiked during the day

**Target account tiers:**
- 15–20 replies on large accounts (50K+ followers) — pure visibility
- 15–20 replies on medium accounts (5K–50K) — relationship and follow-back potential
- 10 replies on emerging accounts in your niche — loyal follower acquisition

**Prioritize finding threads that are currently blowing up** (1K+ likes in the last hour). A well-crafted reply on a thread gaining momentum is worth 10× more than a reply on an old post.

### Engagement Velocity Checklist (Post-Publish Priming)

Do this within 5 minutes of every post:

1. **Reply to your own post** with a follow-up thought, question, or additional context → signals conversation to the algorithm
2. **Drop the post in 1–2 relevant Communities** → community notification = additional early views
3. **Send to 1–2 genuine connections** in DMs (not mass spam) → personal reach for early engagement
4. **Monitor for 30 minutes** → reply to every comment that comes in during the first engagement window

If a post starts getting traction (5+ likes in first 15 min), reply to every single early commenter to extend the engagement cascade.

### X Communities Strategy

- Join 3–5 communities in your niche (search "[niche] community" on X)
- Post every thread and most standalone posts to at least one community
- Engage within communities — reply to other posts, not just your own drops
- Communities to post in daily: your primary niche + 1 adjacent niche community

### X Spaces (Optional but High-ROI)

- Host one 30–45 min Space per week on a niche topic starting Week 2
- Invite 2–3 accounts with 1K+ followers in your niche to co-host
- All attendees see you listed as a speaker — organic discovery
- Particularly effective for finance, AI, and productivity niches

## Shadowban Detection & Recovery

### How to Check if You're Shadowbanned
1. Log out of X (or use incognito)
2. Search for a recent distinctive tweet by your exact words
3. Search for your @username
4. If your posts don't appear when logged out but do when logged in → you have a search shadowban

Tools: shadowban.yuzurisa.com

### Types
| Type | Symptom | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Search ban | Posts invisible in search | 24–48 hours |
| Ghost ban | Invisible in For You / replies | 48–72 hours |
| Reply ban | Replies hidden under posts | 24–72 hours |
| Full suspension | Account locked | Review required |

### Recovery
1. Stop all posting immediately
2. Identify and stop the triggering behavior (usually automation, follow-unfollow, or rate limit breach)
3. Do not post for 48 hours minimum
4. Return to natural, human-paced behavior — 1–2 posts, a few genuine replies
5. Shadowbans typically lift in 48–72 hours with no triggering behavior
