## Use Cases

### 1. Support Agent → Founder / Support Lead

**Persona:** 2-person SaaS startup; founder doubles as support.
**Task AM handles:** Routes and responds to inbound support tickets 24/7, escalates edge cases, logs resolved patterns to a knowledge base.
**Measurable outcome:** 50% reduction in support overhead; overnight and weekend coverage without a hire.
**Grounded in:** Tidio and Lindy.ai case studies — 50% support overhead reduction cited in Lindy.ai/blog/ai-agents-business-applications.

---

### 2. Sales Development Rep → Founder / Growth Lead

**Persona:** Solo founder running outbound alongside everything else.
**Task AM handles:** Qualifies inbound leads, routes them to the right stage in the pipeline, sends follow-up sequences, and updates the CRM — end-to-end.
**Measurable outcome:** Equivalent to avoiding one SDR FTE per five active consultants; zero leads fall through the cracks.
**Grounded in:** Research finding that AI agents enable "avoiding one FTE per five consultants" in sales development workflows.

---

### 3. Marketing Coordinator → Content Founder / Indie Hacker

**Persona:** 1-person B2B product studio, shipping content weekly.
**Task AM handles:** Drafts long-form posts from bullet outlines, repurposes into social threads, schedules and tracks distribution, reports on engagement.
**Measurable outcome:** Reclaims 15–20 hrs/week consumed by marketing — based on observed solo founder time spend on content production.
**Grounded in:** Research pain point #3: "15–20 hrs/week consumed by marketing alone" (research.md).

---

### 4. Engineering Teammate → Solo Developer / Technical Founder

**Persona:** Technical founder building a product solo.
**Task AM handles:** Code generation, PR review, writing tests, keeping a kanban board current, and committing auditable changes with full context preserved.
**Measurable outcome:** 55–60% reduction in dev cycle time; persistent context means no re-explaining the codebase on every session.
**Grounded in:** GitHub Copilot data — "up to 55% productivity boost"; Cursor implied ship-speed benchmarks. AM adds memory + git auditability on top.

---

### 5. Operations Coordinator → Ops-less Team

**Persona:** 3-person team running multiple async projects with no ops hire.
**Task AM handles:** Tracks project state on a kanban board, surfaces blockers, writes meeting notes and async updates, maintains documentation as a living record.
**Measurable outcome:** 21+ hrs/quarter saved on coordination; eliminates the "founder as bottleneck" failure mode where decisions queue on one person.
**Grounded in:** Research pain point #4 ("founder as bottleneck") and meeting/async coordination stat: "21+ hrs/quarter saved" (research.md).

---

### 6. Financial Analyst → Bootstrapped Founder

**Persona:** Bootstrapped SaaS founder without a bookkeeper or CFO.
**Task AM handles:** Auto-categorizes transactions, prepares monthly summaries, flags anomalies, and assembles tax-ready reports.
**Measurable outcome:** Eliminates the need for a part-time bookkeeper ($600–$1K/month); closes the books monthly without manual effort.
**Grounded in:** Research ROI area #6: "Financial operations — auto-categorization, tax prep without a bookkeeper" (research.md); cost frame sourced from entrepreneurloop.com.
