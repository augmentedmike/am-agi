# YouTube 2026: 30-Day Growth Plan (0 → Max Subscribers)

## Niche Selection

**Chosen niche: AI Agent Engineering & System Architecture (sub-niche: building production-grade AI agents in public)**

**Rationale:**
- Highest engagement rates in tech content: builder-in-public, raw engineering, and "what actually breaks" content has strong watch time
- Low–medium competition at the sub-niche level: most AI channels target end-users; "production agent engineering" is underserved
- CPM $10–$25 — engineering content monetizes at the high end
- Naturally produces both Shorts content (quick debugging clips, architecture diagrams) and long-form (full system walkthroughs)
- Cross-platform repurposing is easy: architecture hot takes work on X, raw coding clips work on TikTok
- Strong search volume: "agent state management", "prompt injection protection", "worktree isolation", "gated state machine" all have growing demand from the engineering audience

**Channel positioning:** "Engineering production-grade AI agents in public — showing what actually breaks" — technical depth, raw build footage, no fluff.

## Early YPP Milestone Plan (500 Subscribers)

At **500 subscribers**, early YPP access unlocks:
- **Community Posts** — start posting polls and teasers immediately; pre-warm audiences before uploads
- **Super Thanks** — viewers can tip on videos
- **Channel Memberships** — offer exclusive content or early access
- **Merchandise Shelf** — add affiliate/merch links

Action at 500 subs:
1. Apply for early YPP immediately
2. Launch Community Posts within 24 hours of approval
3. Set up a membership tier (even $2.99/month) with one benefit (early access to next video)
4. Pin a "Support the channel" community post to celebrate the milestone — converts the momentum of the milestone into action

Standard YPP (ad revenue) remains at 1,000 subs / 4,000 watch hours — track progress weekly.
