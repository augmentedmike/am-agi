# YouTube 2026: 30-Day Growth Plan (0 → Max Subscribers)

## Niche Selection

**Chosen niche: AI Tools & Tutorials (sub-niche: practical AI workflows for everyday people)**

**Rationale:**
- Highest YoY growth of any niche (+340%), meaning the algorithm is already pushing this content to hungry audiences
- Low–medium competition at the sub-niche level: most AI channels target developers; "AI for normal people" is underserved
- CPM $8–$20 — monetizes well once threshold is hit
- Naturally produces both Shorts content (quick AI tips, before/after demos) and long-form (full workflow walkthroughs)
- Cross-platform repurposing is easy: AI tool demos work on TikTok and Reels with no adaptation
- Strong search volume: "how to use ChatGPT", "best AI tools 2026", "AI image generator tutorial" all have massive search demand

**Channel positioning:** "The AI shortcuts your boss doesn't know about" — practical, time-saving, no jargon.

## Early YPP Milestone Plan (500 Subscribers)

At **500 subscribers**, early YPP access unlocks:
- **Community Posts** — start posting polls and teasers immediately; pre-warm audiences before uploads
- **Super Thanks** — viewers can tip on videos
- **Channel Memberships** — offer exclusive content or early access
- **Merchandise Shelf** — add affiliate/merch links

Action at 500 subs:
1. Apply for early YPP immediately
2. Launch Community Posts within 24 hours of approval
3. Set up a membership tier (even $2.99/month) with one benefit (early access to next video)
4. Pin a "Support the channel" community post to celebrate the milestone — converts the momentum of the milestone into action

Standard YPP (ad revenue) remains at 1,000 subs / 4,000 watch hours — track progress weekly.

## Key Metrics to Track Weekly

| Metric | Target | Action if Below |
|---|---|---|
| CTR | > 5% | Swap thumbnail; test new title |
| Average View Duration | > 40% | Improve hooks; cut slow intros |
| Subscriber Conversion Rate | > 1% of views | Add stronger CTAs; improve value delivery |
| Shorts Completion Rate | > 80% | Shorten; cut slower opening 3 seconds |
| Comment Rate | > 1% of views | Ask better questions; reply faster |
