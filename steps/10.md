# Step 10: New Card Panel

The board needs a way to create cards without touching the CLI. A single accent-colored button on the top bar opens a panel that slides down from the top. The panel has one input: a textarea for the work description. Submit creates the card in backlog and the panel rolls back up.

One accent color throughout. No modal overlay — the panel slides in from the top bar itself, not the center of the screen.

---

## Prompt

```
add a [new card] button on the top bar right side. use the existing pink accent color. clicking it slides a panel down from the top bar (not a modal — it extends downward from the bar). the panel has a single textarea for the work description. the textarea grows vertically to fit the text — no fixed height, no scrollbar. a [create] button submits: POST /api/cards with the textarea content as the title. on success the panel slides back up and the card appears in backlog. clicking the button again when open closes the panel (toggle). no other fields — title only for now.
```

---

## Implementation Notes for AM

Read steps/10.md and CLAUDE.md before starting.

**What to build:**

Top bar button (`apps/board/src/components/BoardClient.tsx`):
- Add a `[new card]` button to the right side of the top bar
- Use the existing pink accent: `bg-pink-500 hover:bg-pink-400 text-white`
- Toggles `panelOpen` state on click

New card panel (`apps/board/src/components/NewCardPanel.tsx` — new file):
- Renders below the top bar, `overflow-hidden` with `max-h-0` → `max-h-96` transition for slide-down/up effect
- Contains a single `<textarea>` — no label needed, placeholder: `"Describe the work..."`
- Textarea sizing: `resize-none overflow-hidden` with an `onInput` handler that sets `el.style.height = 'auto'` then `el.style.height = el.scrollHeight + 'px'` — grows with content, no scrollbar
- A `[create]` button: same pink accent, right-aligned below the textarea
- On create: POST to `/api/cards` with `{ title: textareaValue }`, clear textarea, close panel
- Glass background: `bg-zinc-900/95 backdrop-blur-md border-b border-white/10 px-6 py-4`

Board layout:
- Panel sits between the top bar and the column area — not overlapping content
- After successful create, the cards list refreshes (re-fetch or optimistic insert into backlog column)

**Definition of done:**
- `[new card]` button visible in top bar, pink accent
- Clicking opens panel sliding down from top bar
- Clicking again closes it
- Textarea grows with content, no scrollbar
- `[create]` submits and closes panel
- New card appears in backlog without page reload
- `bun test` passes
- No TypeScript errors from new code
